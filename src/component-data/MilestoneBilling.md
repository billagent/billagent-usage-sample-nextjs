# Milestone Services Agreement

This Milestone Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of **March 12, 2026** ("Contract Signed Date") by and between:

- **Vendor:** OnsiteIQ ("Vendor")
- **Customer:** Edward Rose & Sons ("Customer")

Vendor and Customer may be referred to individually as a "Party" and collectively as the "Parties".

---

## 1. Term and Project Timeline

- **1.1 Term.** The term of this Agreement shall commence on **March 12, 2026** and continue through **February 28, 2028**, unless earlier terminated as provided herein.
- **1.2 Project Start Date.** The project start date shall be March 1, 2026 unless the Parties mutually confirm a different date in writing.
- **1.3 Project Completion.** The project will be deemed complete upon (a) Vendor's delivery of the final deliverables as defined in Section 2 and (b) Customer's acceptance per Section 6, which shall occur no later than February 28, 2028 unless extended by a mutually executed change order.

## 2. Scope of Work and Deliverables

**2.1 Scope.** Vendor will provide the services and deliverables described below (the "Services" and "Deliverables"):

- **Summary of Scope:** OnsiteIQ to scan construction site 1 and construction site 2
- **Deliverables:** Signed agreement for scope of work; scan construction site 1; scan construction site 2
- **Milestone Criteria:**
  - 10% of contract value due upon signed agreement for scope of work
  - 45% of contract value due when project starts for scan construction site 1
  - 45% due when project is completed for scan construction site 2

**2.2 Assumptions & Dependencies.** The Parties acknowledge that the schedule and fees assume timely access to Customer personnel, systems, and data, and prompt review and approvals.

## 3. Fees and Payment Schedule

- **3.1 Total Contract Value.** The total contract value is **$10,000** (ten thousand U.S. dollars), exclusive of applicable taxes.

**3.2 Milestone Payments.** Fees are due as follows:

| Milestone | Trigger | % of Total | Amount | Due |
|-----------|---------|------------|--------|-----|
| 1 – Contract Signing | Executed agreement | 10% | $1,000 | March 12 , 2026 |
| 2 – Project Start | Project Start Date | 45% | $4,500 | June 16, 2026 |
| 3 – Project Completion | All deliverables accepted; invoice issued | 45% | $4,500 | Upon completion February 28, 2028|

- **3.3 Invoicing & Payment Terms.** Vendor will issue invoices upon each milestone. Invoices are due **Net 15** days from invoice date. Late payments accrue interest at the lesser of **1.5% per month** or the maximum allowed by law.
- **3.4 Taxes.** Fees are exclusive of taxes. Customer is responsible for applicable taxes except for taxes based on Vendor's net income.

## 4. Change Management

- **4.1 Change Orders.** Adjustments to scope, timeline, or deliverables require a written change order signed by both Parties.

## 5. Customer Responsibilities

- **5.1 Access & Resources.** Customer will provide timely access to necessary systems, personnel, and information.
- **5.2 Review & Approval.** Customer will review deliverables within **five (5) business days** unless otherwise agreed.

## 6. Acceptance

- **6.1 Acceptance Procedure.** Vendor will notify Customer when a milestone Deliverable is complete. Customer will have five (5) business days to accept or provide documented reasons for rejection based on agreed criteria.
- **6.2 Deemed Acceptance.** If Customer does not respond within the review period, the Deliverable shall be deemed accepted.
